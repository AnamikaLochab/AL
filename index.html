Hi, I am Anamika Lochab.

I'm currently pursuing my PhD in Computer Science at Purdue University under the guidance of Prof. Ruqi Zhang. Previously, I completed my Master's degree in Computer Science at Rutgers University, where I received an Outstanding Project Award. I earned my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Vellore Institute of Technology, Bhopal, graduating as a Gold Medallist.

My research focuses on trustworthy and robust AI, particularly in aligning large language models (LLMs) through learning from human feedback. I also explore multimodal alignment and model uncertainty to improve generalization and safety across diverse model families.
